Addressing the Grading Challenge

Modern educators face increasing administrative burdens. AWA was developed to bridge the gap between submission and feedback, specifically for the Microsoft Teams ecosystem.

Time Efficiency

Drastically reduces the time spent navigating menus and downloading files individually.

Format Versatility

Standardizes various submission formats (PDF, Images) into a single, readable view.

Class Management

Designed to handle large classrooms without compromising the quality of individual feedback.

Workflow Integration

AWA integrates seamlessly with your existing Microsoft Teams workflow. No complex setup or migration required.
